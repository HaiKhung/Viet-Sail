Sailing & Sunset Tour

The adventure starts from a very beautiful place in the north west of Phu Quoc . Only 4km away from Cambodia, this quiet place is a small hidden paradise, not yet touristic, with fisherman, floating houses, nice beaches and coral reefs. Enjoy the sea view with natural landscapes and the close up view of the Cambodian islands. This is one of the best areas for sailing in Phu Quoc.

Tour Time

You decide (Sunset at 5 PM)

Start Time

From 8 AM to 5 PM

Price

500K VND / Hour / Person 20 USD / Hour / Person

Tour include

A Friendly tourguide speaking English/ French/ Vietnamese. Life jacket, waterproof phone bag, snorkeling equipment, shoes.
