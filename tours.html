Our Tours Offers

The adventure starts from a very beautiful place in the north west of Phu Quoc . Only 4km away from Cambodia, this quiet place is a small hidden paradise, not yet touristic, with fisherman, floating houses, nice beaches and coral reefs. Enjoy the sea view with natural landscapes and the close up view of the Cambodian islands. This is one of the best areas for sailing in Phu Quoc.

We have 2 different spots for snorkeling in Ganh Dau, depending of the weather :

- The Cap : This is the closest point to the Cambodian islands, the view is amazing, there are beautiful corals and unique rock formations from which we can have a jump into the water.

- The Monkey Beach : A very quiet place where often monkeys can be seen in the trees. There are many huge corals and tropical fishes. This location is recommended for the children.
