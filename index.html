Individual, unique, ecological.

The sailboat remains the most responsible way to navigate and explore the coasts and islands. It requires a very modest use of the engine and generates low CO2 emissions. The sailing boat is also a silent means of transport, no noise pollution.

We also adopt environmentally friendly behaviour to preserve marine biodiversity and ensure its sustainability. The sea is a rich space to be preserved. The seabed is fragile and anchoring can damage certain protected species. We choose an anchorage area according to the bottom and drop our anchor on a light-coloured sandy area.
