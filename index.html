Learn Sailing In The Vietnamese Island Paradise

Individual, unique, ecological.

As the first sailing centre on Phu Quoc island we aim to inspire the community to participate in tours and training and feel the real sailing spirit.
... Our instructors and tour guides are highly qualified and experienced experts in their field.
Furthermore we partner up with local associations and schools to initiate and support social and ecological projects.
Come sail away with us! Book your preferred offer and chill, take pictures & videos, listen to music, swim, snorkel, learn skills or use the trapeze and fly above the water!
How can we serve you today - relaxation or adventure? The choice is all yours
